International Space Station Distance Calculator











What does this website do?

This website calculates the distance between your location and the International Space Station (ISS) projected on the Earth's surface, based on the address provided.

You may leave some unknown parts of the address blank, but the precision of the calculation may decrease.

The distance is calculated by obtaining the ISS current coordinates through its own API, transform the provided address to coordinates with googlemaps, and finally calculate the distance using Haversine formula.
